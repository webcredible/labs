Let’s design a better world, together

The Labs is the space & time for Webcredible to work on innovative ideas outside regular client work




So what are we exactly doing, and why?

Launch 1-2 projects before the end of August 2015.

A project can be a proof of concept, a minisite that tracks a piece of information in a beautiful way, a whitepaper published in a major managinze, a service that can be sold for a profit, whatever you like.

 
  • An opportunity to learn new skills & techniques
  • Gain empathy on how our clients work
  • Add exciting work to our personal & company portfolios


What about an example?

 

Elections are coming.

Soon we'll be bombarded with tons of information about what the different parties are doing. But, how can we differenciate the signal from the noise?

A potential Labs project could involve some clever way to extract langauge patterns from the different leaders. Or visualise where the tweets about the elections are coming from. Or...


How are we going to select the ideas?

1 Generate many ideas.
Post ideas in the kitchen whiteboard, anything that you consider interesting. Remeber, just because you posted something doesn't mean you're responsible for developing it! Others may kidnap and expand your idea.

2 Elevator pitch.
2 minutes each idea, to explain why is good, how is going to make Webcredible famous, and the design/build feasability.

When: Friday 6th February

Download the proposition canvas to brainstorm ideas.

3 Pitch to the Dragon's Den.
The selected ideas will have 5 man-days to get ready, and then 20 minutes to pitch to Trenton, Pete, Gemma and Nirish.

Sell it! Tell them who's going to work on it, the plan, the milestones. How are you going to promote it. Your partners (if you have any), risks, etc.

When: Friday 20th February

And then, how do we develop it?

We plan to announced the winning idea the Monday 23rd February. Then you'll have at least 30 man-days to create and launch that idea. How you spend that time is up to you (remember, you're presenting a plan to the panel!). On top of that, you can use the Marketing team to promote it. If you need expernal resources, just pitch for it.

And remember, the mentors are here to help you make this a reality.
